Situation Reaction Test

Instructions
  • 01A situation will appear on screen for 30 seconds.
  • 02Write your immediate reaction on paper — what he would do.
  • 03Always write in third person ("He did...").
  • 04Keep it short — 1 to 2 lines only. First instinct wins.
  • 05Your hero must be positive, practical, and action-oriented.
  • 06A buzzer sounds at 30 seconds. Next situation appears automatically.
